About Typo Dojo

Our Mission

Typo Dojo was created to make typing practice more effective and personalized. We believe that everyone can improve their typing skills with the right approach‚Äîone that focuses on your specific weaknesses rather than generic exercises.

How It Works

Typo Dojo uses advanced pattern recognition to identify the specific characters, character pairs, and sequences where you make mistakes. Instead of practicing random text, you'll focus on passages that contain more of the patterns you struggle with, helping you break bad habits faster.

Our algorithm tracks errors at multiple levels‚Äîfrom individual characters to quadgrams‚Äîensuring comprehensive coverage of your typing patterns. This data-driven approach means every practice session is tailored to help you improve where it matters most.

Why Typo Dojo?

Traditional typing tutors often use the same exercises for everyone, regardless of skill level or specific challenges. Typo Dojo adapts to you:

  • Personalized Practice: Exercises are generated based on your actual typing patterns
  • Real-time Feedback: See your progress with detailed statistics and metrics
  • Privacy-First: All data is stored locally in your browser
  • Free Forever: No subscriptions, no paywalls, just effective typing practice

Our Story

Typo Dojo was born from frustration with existing typing practice tools. We wanted something that would actually learn from our mistakes and help us improve efficiently. After building it for ourselves, we realized others might benefit too‚Äîso we made it free and open to everyone.

Today, Typo Dojo helps over 280,000 people improve their typing skills. We're constantly working to make it better, adding new features and refining our algorithms based on user feedback.
